
Enter, Left Mouse Up = Show
ESC = Hide
Canvas Enter - Show if activated
Canvas Leave - Hide

H = ScrollWheel Adjusts Width, Mouse is not tracked
V = ScrollWheel Adjusts Height

M = Position of center tracks mouse, ScrollWheel adjust size, keeping aspect ratio
	If mouse moves further left than 1/2 width of rectangle + left side of canvas, rectangle is positioned so it's left side is at positon 0.
